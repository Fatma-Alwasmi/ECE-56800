* Pin 37 (GPIO37) is connected to the output pin of the potentiometer 
* Pin 13 (LED) is connected to the LED
* Pin 3V (3.3V) is connected to the right pin (VCC) of the potentiometer 
* Pin GND is connected to the left pin (GND) of the potentiometer and the cathode pin on the LED
